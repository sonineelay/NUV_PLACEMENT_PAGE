Career Development Center @ NUV

Navrachana University has always remained a preferred destination to hire fresh talent because of its highly professional student quality, industry-aligned curriculum, highly qualified & experienced faculties, Research facilities and strong industry connections. Navrachana University's placement process is designed to make recruitment easy and flexible to optimize students' success. We help students find the right opportunities based on their interests and aptitude and by matching them with recruiting organizations' requirements. A thoughtfully designed and carefully nurtured vibrant campus culture ensures that students turn into well-rounded and sought-after professionals, while experiencing some of the best times of their lives. With more than 500+ national and multinational recruiters on board, NUVites have plenty to choose from, if they don't become job creators themselves. So, WHEN YOU ARE AT NUV, YOU ARE #WellPlaced
